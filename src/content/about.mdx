## About Me

Hi everyone! I am a UX/UI designer with over 14 years of experience in projects for the e-commerce, pharmaceutical, and national government industry. Skilled in using Figma, Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, SASS and JavaScript. Passionate about the latest technological trends and web design.

I work at the intersection of design and frontend development — I don't just hand off specs, I build the things I design. That hybrid profile lets me create interfaces that are both beautiful and technically sound.

Based in **Buenos Aires, Argentina** 🇦🇷

---

## Core Philosophy

> *Translating complex logic into intuitive human experiences.*

Design is not decoration — it's a communication system. Every color, every spacing choice, every micro-interaction is a deliberate decision aimed at reducing friction and increasing clarity.

I believe in:

- **Systems over one-offs** — scalable design beats one-time solutions
- **Code-aware design** — understanding constraints makes better designers  
- **Dark mode first** — because good contrast is good design
- **Motion with purpose** — animation should inform, not distract
