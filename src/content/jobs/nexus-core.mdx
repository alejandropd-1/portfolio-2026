## The Challenge

Nexus Financial approached us with a monolithic legacy system that was alienating their younger demographic. The core challenge was to design an interface that conveyed institutional-grade security while maintaining the fluid, instantaneous feel of modern consumer apps.

We needed to reduce cognitive load on complex portfolio screens without hiding essential market data points.

### Dark Mode as Default

We pivoted the entire brand aesthetic to a deep, OLED-friendly dark mode. This wasn't just aesthetic; it reduced eye strain for day traders spending 8+ hours in the app, and allowed critical syntax highlights (red/green market indicators) to pop with maximum contrast.
